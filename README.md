JobCloud AIO & GEO Content Scorer
A browser-based tool for evaluating article content against AIO (AI Optimisation) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) criteria. Designed to help content teams assess how well their writing is structured for retrieval by AI-powered search engines and generative answer tools.
How to use
Open the tool in any browser. Paste your article text directly, or upload an HTML file. The scorer analyses your content across seven dimensions and returns a weighted overall score along with specific recommendations.
What it measures
Structural Clarity, Direct Answer Signals, Factual Density, Content Completeness, Question Coverage, Semantic Richness, and Readability (including Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level with component breakdown).
No setup required
The tool runs entirely in the browser. No data is sent to any server, no account is needed, and nothing is stored.
